General Tips and Tricks

1. Take your Dog for a walk

It seems common sense, I know, but getting out of the house with your
dog can increase its overall happiness - not to mention, trim your waistline!

2. Play with your dog

Much like the first on our list, playing with your pup often can
invoke feelings of happiness within your dogs subconcious! Just remember
not to play too rough!

3. Give them something to chew on

Now for something your dog will really like! Giving your dog something
to chew on can help promote good teeth hygiene - keeping those pearly whites
clean and healthy!

4. Protect your dogs from potential dangers

By keeping potential pests away from your dog - such as fleas, ticks, and other
harmful insects, you can keep them from scratching and spreading them into your home.

5. Take your dog to the veternarian often

It is extremely important to take your dog to the veternarian often. Make sure
you tell them about changes to your dogs diet and any other behavior changes.

6. Make sure to have clean water available

This one is kind of obvious, but what a lot of people don't seem to understand is that
bacteria can form in water. This means you should be changing your dogs water as often
as possible.

7. Train your dog in the basics

This tip can be easier when they are younger, but it's still a pain. It's important to train
your dog in the basic commands - studies have shown that dog owners that train their dogs often
report their dog living a long and safe life.
